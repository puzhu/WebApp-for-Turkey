Visualization of enterprise survey data from Turkey

This dashboard visualizes enterprise survey data that was collected during the period 2015-16. The data was collected to be representative, for each of the 108 indicators that are split between 13 topics, at the three regional levels (Nuts 1, Nut 2, and Nuts 3) and 18 sub-groups.

Along with basic actions such as selecting, a new topic (tabs) or indicator (dropdown menu) or the different regional levels (Nuts 1, Nut 2, and Nuts 3), users can dive deep into this data by interacting with the charts. Hovering on a region on the map will trigger changes on the bar chart, which will show the split of the data between the different sub-groups for that particular region (instead of just aggregate estimates for all of Turkey). Similary, hovering over the bar chart will allow users to observe the regional variations for a particular sub-grouping, for instance, the percentage of female led firms that experience bribe payments across different regions
